The Best Movies in the World

Here are Will's favorite movies ;)

Castaway

Castaway is an excellent movie. Tom Hanks does an excellent job portraying the descent into madness due to isolation. I also love the character Wilson. Overall 10/10 Movie.

Shutter Island

Phenomenal Film with a crazy plot twist at the end. The atmosphere in this movie is so mysterious and ominous - I love it!

Get Out

This movie is awesome, and directed by Jordan Peele. Very captivating plot, that makes it more than just another horror movie. I highly recommend.
